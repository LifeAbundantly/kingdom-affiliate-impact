Overview

The ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is not just a platform—it's a revolutionary movement. As we navigate this blue ocean of opportunity, we boldly declare that the Kingdom of God is here, and we are empowered to partner with ABBA in His wealth transfer. Together, we are stewards of the wealth transfer, catalyzing a movement that impacts the industry and the world, one generous act at a time.

Phase 1: Breaking Chains of Poverty and Building a Kingdom Foundation

In the first phase, our goal is to bring together 1,000 Kingdom members in the first 30 days. Each donor’s $1,222 seed sown represents more than just financial generosity; it represents a radical shift in the lives of children and families bound by poverty. These 1,000 initial donors will directly empower the transformation of lives, bringing financial freedom and spiritual renewal to many.

As these 1,000 donors step out in faith, they will contribute to the breaking of generational poverty. Their generosity will fuel the Kingdom mission, creating a ripple effect of empowerment and abundance. These initial donations will help fund projects that establish self-sustaining communities, educate, and provide for those who have long been without.
