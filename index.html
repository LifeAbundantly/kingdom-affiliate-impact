ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Empowering Generosity, Reaching Nations, Transforming Lives for Jesus

Overview

ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is rooted in Messiah Jesus—crucified, buried, and risen on the third day. This program is an invitation to live the Gospel, fostering radical generosity inspired by the Holy Spirit. It combines charitable giving with Kingdom-building innovation, creating opportunities for believers to impact the world spiritually and practically while experiencing the abundant life Jesus promised.

Program Reach and Demographics

The program seeks to engage both believers and those searching for truth, with an emphasis on empowering:

  • Global Christian Population: Reaching over **2.5 billion Christians**, with a projected impact of **12.5 million participants** annually at a 0.5% penetration rate.
  • Passive Income Seekers: Engaging individuals already involved in or seeking opportunities in the growing affiliate marketing industry, projected to reach **$20 billion** globally by 2025.
  • Primary Demographic: Active Christians, aged 25–55, with a heart for giving and moderate to high financial capacity.
  • Secondary Demographic: Faith-based communities and individuals exploring passive income opportunities.
