Messiah Jesus Kingdom Mission

Empowering Communities Through Generosity Rooted in Jesus

Overview

This Kingdom Mission reflects Messiah Jesus' call to love and serve others as He demonstrated on the cross—crucified, buried, and resurrected for our redemption. The program invites believers and unbelievers to align with Kingdom principles of giving, spiritual growth, and communal transformation, advancing the glory of Jesus and His resurrection through generosity and stewardship.

Updated Giving Breakdown

Our foundational system operates on these Kingdom-based principles:

  • Main Link Contributions: - 58% for Kingdom Mission (serving widows, orphans, and the poor). - 42% for Program Development and Builders (split equally into 14% allocations for growth, innovation, and leadership stewardship).
  • Affiliate Contributions: - 90% returned to donors through reaping rewards. - 9% directed to SEO Kingdom Mission. - 1% invested into Co-Creator initiatives.

Target Demographics and Reach

We aim to reach individuals across the world, empowering them with the message of Messiah Jesus and His redemptive mission:

  • Primary Demographic: Active Christians aged 25–55, representing 58% of participants.
  • Secondary Demographic: Passive income seekers, unbelieving believers, and those curious about Kingdom-based stewardship (42%).
  • Online Income Community: Attracting individuals actively engaged in affiliate marketing or seeking financial empowerment through Kingdom values.
