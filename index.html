Overview

The ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is more than a platform—it is a Holy Spirit-led mission to advance the Kingdom of God through generosity, faithfulness, and abundance. Rooted in Jesus Christ as the cornerstone, this program provides a groundbreaking way for participants to sow into God’s work while receiving financial empowerment and spiritual transformation. With every seed sown, we are building a storehouse outpouring, designed to bless communities globally and fulfill the call for wealth transfer as promised in the Word of God.

Phased Growth Goals

Phase 1: Building the Foundation

Our first phase focuses on establishing the program’s foundation by mobilizing 1,000 members within the first 30 days. These members will become Kingdom partners and foundational stewards, aligning their giving with God’s Kingdom vision.

Phase 2: Expanding Outreach

By month six, the program will aim for a 6x increase in members, fueled by Holy Spirit guidance and the program’s growth through a powerful network of referrals. This phase focuses on community building and establishing trust through testimonies and visible Kingdom impact.

Phase 3: Multiplying Generosity

In the first year, we will scale to over 58,000 members. This phase is designed to multiply generosity and expand the reach of the program’s mission to underserved communities globally.

Phase 4: Global Penetration

By Year 2, the program targets a 17% penetration across all demographics, with over 183,000 members. This phase is marked by exponential growth, creating a worldwide ripple effect of generosity.
