Program Overview

Inspired by the generosity of the early church in the Book of Acts, ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program redefines giving through a sustainable, Holy Spirit-driven model. This initiative empowers participants to experience financial freedom, fuel Kingdom missions, and create a lasting ripple effect of generosity. At its core is a one-time donation of $1,222, followed by dynamic affiliate-based reaping and automated payouts.
