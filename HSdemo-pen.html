ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Impact Analysis and Growth Projections

Overview

The ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program revolutionizes faith-based giving by merging Holy Spirit-inspired generosity with passive income opportunities. Based on principles of sowing and reaping, this program empowers participants across all demographics, creating a global ripple effect of Kingdom impact.

Expanded Demographics and Growth Potential

The program targets high school-aged young adults, young adults, and Christians of all ages, leveraging their unique strengths to achieve exponential growth. Here's a breakdown of key demographics:

  • High School Students (14–18): 600 million globally.
  • Young Adults (18–24): 400 million globally.
  • Christian Adults (25–55): 1 billion globally.
  • Passive/Online Income Seekers: 200 million globally.

Initial penetration is projected at 0.5% across all demographics, with growth accelerating to 17% within 7–10 years.
